Biography

William Shakespeare (1564-1616) was an English playwright, poet, and actor, widely regarded as the greatest writer in the English language and the world's greatest dramatist. His works capture the complexities of human nature and profound psychological insight.

Born in Stratford-upon-Avon, Shakespeare began his career in London. His legacy includes masterpieces such as Hamlet, Romeo and Juliet, Macbeth, and Othello.
